Understanding Food Allergies

A food allergy is a serious medical condition where the immune system overreacts to a food protein. Reactions can be severe and even life-threatening.

30-40%

of the world's population suffers from one or more allergies.

32 Million

Americans suffer from food allergies, including 5.6 million children.

$25 Billion

in annual healthcare costs worldwide are attributed to food allergies.
