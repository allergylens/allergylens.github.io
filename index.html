Understanding Food Allergies

⚠️

Immune System Overreaction

Food allergies occur when the immune system mistakenly identifies harmless food proteins as threats, triggering potentially dangerous reactions.

🩺

Varied Symptoms

Symptoms can range from mild to life-threatening, including throat swelling, difficulty breathing, vomiting, and other serious reactions.

🚨

Life-Threatening Risks

Severe allergic reactions can be fatal, making accurate ingredient identification absolutely critical for safety.

Challenges with Food Labels

📄

Dense Text

Ingredient lists are often printed in tiny fonts and packed with complex terms that are difficult to read quickly.

⚠️

Cross Contamination

Warning labels about potential cross-contamination can be unclear or inconsistent across products.

🌍

Foreign Languages

International products may have labels in languages you don't understand, making ingredient identification impossible.
